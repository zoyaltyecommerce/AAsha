Accommodation/patient rooms

Sophisticated medical care is very essential these days, but nevertheless, it is essential that accommodation provided must be equally attractive for the patient to feel comfortable. Aasha Hospital has gone that extra way to provide a room environ that will promote the well-being and quick recovery of the patient. No compromise has been made in incurring cost on accommodation as Aasha Hospital has felt the need to lay much focus on patient care.

Pharmacy

Pharmacy is an essential component of the hospital. In-house pharmacy facility certainly makes it easy for the patients to get their medications within the hospital. There is no need for the relatives to move out of the hospital. In an emergency, it is easy to take care of the patient, if the medicine is available immediately, then certainly, it is easy to provide excellent medical care. After all, it is a question of life and death for the patient.
